Commissioning Persian Calligraphy: Styles, Pricing & Process

If you have a Persian poem or phrase that you would like to bring to life, we can create a custom calligraphy artwork either in your choice of Nasta ølƒ´q or Siyah Mashq (the abstract, expressive form) to add depth and beauty to your artistic collection. To place your order, please follow the structure below. If you are still searching for the perfect verse, one that resonates with your dream, life purpose, or legacy, we invite you to schedule a complimentary consultation session. Together, we‚Äôll explore verses that align with your journey and offer personalized guidance to help you discover the words that are truly yours.

Our commissioned artworks unite two of the most cherished traditions in Persian art: calligraphy and illumination (Tazhib). Through a deeply collaborative process, SaBa, the calligrapher, and Narvan, the master of illumination, merge their distinct crafts into a single, harmonious masterpiece. SaBa sets the foundation with expressive flowing script, while Narvan enhances the piece with intricate geometric patterns, elegant floral motifs, and finely detailed miniature elements. Together, they transform the written word into a visual composition tailored to reflect your unique taste and vision.

How to Place Your Art Commission

To initiate your art commission, please follow the steps outlined below.

  • Share your favorite text with us, whether it's a phrase, a line of poetry, or a meaningful verse. If you'd like help finding the perfect phrase, we're happy to guide you and offer a curated selection of verses that align with your taste and vision.
  • Select your preferred calligraphy style, Nasta ølƒ´q or Siyah Mashq. Refer to the images for a visual representation of each technique.
  • Fill out the commission sheet and email it to saba@sabapersiancalligraphy.com to begin your custom artwork.
  • The quoted price includes your commissioned artwork created on 100% cotton fine art paper for small and medium sizes and on premium canvas for larger formats.
  • The final pricing is determined based on several factors, including the complexity of the composition, preferred size, passe-partout design, the intricacy of the illumination (Tazhib), and a custom-crafted frame tailored specifically for your artwork.
